MAE CLAIRE IS AN ACTRESS, MODEL, AND SINGER.

ABOUT ME

Mae Claire is a American actress, model, and singer born in the Philippines and is currently based in Philadelphia, PA. She studies the Meisner Technique at Playhouse West acting conservatory with Tony Savant and is well known for roles in local TV/Film productions including ABM DigiFilm's "Catwoman: Retribution" and Nick Gavin's "Don't Dawn", as well as voiceover work for audiobooks, animations, and indie video games such as Illumination Cinema's "Another Day Colder" and Team Galanx's "Konstandin". She has also done commercial work for Overwatch, Belif, Philadelphia Eagles, and Breastcancer.org.   

Since 2013, Mae Claire has been working as a freelance model and is internationally published in several magazines such as KIRAMEKI and ELEGANT.  At a young age, Mae Claire performed as one of the lead singers in her family's band "Weekend Rush" for over 10 years. She also plays several instruments including the piano, guitar, ukulele, and flute. Under the alias of KISA, Mae Claire is a featured singer in DJ Kurono's Japanese electronic music album "KUNOICHI STYLE" released on May 2013.

In her free time, Mae Claire enjoys listening to jazz, reading fantasy novels and Webtoons, and watching horror movies. Mae Claire is also an avid fan of geek pop culture and is heavily influenced by the visuals and stories found in anime, comics, and video games. She has been invited as a cosplay guest to perform, host, and judge at several venues all over the East Coast including InochiCon, AnimeNext, CPAC, KotoriCon, and J1-CON. .


"I've never been that type of person to speak out or stand my ground. I've never felt like I was a strong woman. Now, I want others to be unashamed and unafraid to be brutally honest with themselves and follow through with their passions. But how can I inspire others to do that if I couldn't even do it myself? I really needed to start with myself ."
